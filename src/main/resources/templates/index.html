Project AiAi

The main objective of that project is to innovate the new approach in the relationship between businesses and saving the nature.

In present time we know many companies, which use the images of the nature (i.e. flora, fauna, name of geo locations), make donations for saving of the nature. The most known program is saving tigers program. But there are a lot of company, that use emotional relationship between people and nature for marketing their services and goods, and don't care about of helping to the nature.
